# Sales_dashboard
Developed a sales Excel dashboard utilizing customer purchase data from a store.

> Sales By Category
>
> Pivot table summary of total sales grouped by category
Used for category-wise performance comparison

> Sales By Year
>
> Year-wise aggregation of sales and profit
Helps identify overall growth trends

> Sales By Month
>
> Monthly sales trend analysis
Useful for identifying seasonality patterns

> Top 3 Customers
>
> Identifies the highest-value customers
Based on total sales and profit contribution

> Customer Count
>
> Displays unique customer count
Helps measure customer base size

> Map Chart
>
> Geographic visualization of sales by state
Enables regional performance comparison
